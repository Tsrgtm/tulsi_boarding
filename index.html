Our Story

Tulsi Boarding Secondary School was established in 1987 AD (2044 BS) by "Mr. Bhisma Bahadur Khatri" with the aim to provide quality education to students. Over the decades, it has grown to become a privately owned academic institute located in Tulsipur, Dang (Nepal).

We are affiliated with the (NEB) National Examinations Board and approved by the Ministry of Education, Nepal. Our commitment is to offer Ten plus Two (+2) programs in Science stream, aiming to prepare students for a successful academic and professional journey.

Tulsi Boarding Secondary School, Tulsipur, Dang provides education from the basic to Ten Plus Two (Secondary - +2) level with moderate fee structures. We believe in inclusivity and provide various scholarship schemes for deserving, hardworking, and underprivileged students in our community, ensuring that education remains accessible to all.

Our facilities go beyond the classroom to provide a holistic learning experience for our students. We offer state-of-the-art amenities such as a well-equipped sports complex, an extensive library, a modern computer lab, a fully-equipped science lab, and a range of extra-curricular activities (ECA) to nurture talents beyond academics. We utilize multimedia resources for interactive learning, organize educational tours to enhance practical knowledge, and maintain well-equipped classrooms for a conducive learning environment. We also conduct regular testing to assess students' progress, offer counseling services for their well-being, and provide scholarships to support their educational pursuits.

At Tulsi Boarding Secondary School, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are not only academically competent but also equipped with the skills, values, and knowledge to excel in a rapidly changing world.
